city as system

port of new jersey and new york

a view from my bedroom, as a pedestrian, and as a satellite, seeing all things not just as how they seem to the naked eye, but the systematic, economic, and materialistic manners of "in's and out's."

note: these TEU measurements are aggregated from the PANYNJ yearly reports, and some measurements are estimates or projections gathered from their data. this project is not an exact infographic, but a visualization of the systems forming our environments.
